Leadership

Tanzanian Association in Emirates leaders will constituent of:

  1. Board of Trustee
    Board of trust will include 10 members, founding members of Tanzanian diasporas, Two UAE Nationals and one member of any other country
  2. Leaders of association
    There are ten positions for leaders to be elected on election day. Leaders of the association are the elected members of the association or interim member who temporary cover some position of the leaders for some reasons are not in the country anymore or in case of the resignation or any other unavoidable reason that leave position free. List of the position required to elected by members of TAE are:
    1. The Chairman
    2. The Vice-Chairman
    3. The Secretary
    4. The Deputy Secretary
    5. The Treasurer
    6. The Deputy Treasurer
    7. Four standing committees of the of the Association namely:-
      • Education, Cultural and Social Welfare Committee
      • Budget and Finance Committee
      • Publicity Committee
      • Homeland investment committee
